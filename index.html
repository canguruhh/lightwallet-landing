MyETPWallet

The Metaverse Blockchain lightwallet wallet. Use the Metaverse infrastructure to easily create a digital identity (Avatar) and register your own tokens on the Blockchain.

Open in browser

Features

With the latest supernova upgrade we were able to activate new functions.

Digital Identity

Create your own avatar.

Identifiable Token

Create your own MIT (Metaverse Identifiable Token). A MIT is a single entity record on the Metaverse Blockchain. You can freely exchange it with other avatars.

Certificates

Let your avatar register certificates for MST symbols and domains.

Smart Token

You can easily register your own MST (Metaverse Smart Token) on the Blockchain.

ETP deposit

Lock your ETP for a number of blocks and earn interest.

Multilanguage

The wallet has supports various languages. We are always happy about new translation. Please contact us if you want to support.

About

Opensource

The lightwallet is 100% opensource. We need every helping hand. Please contact us if you want to join our awesome community.

Security

The private keys will only be stored on your own device. All transactions are signed locally.

Speed

Without having to download the whole blockchain your wallet will be ready within the blink of an eye.

Mobile Apps

You can use the browser version but we also have official mobile apps for Android and iOS.
